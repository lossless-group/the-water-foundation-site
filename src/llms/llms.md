# The Water Foundation

> {{SITE_NAME}}

The Water Foundation is a think tank and capital catalyst working toward water independence for 1 billion people by 2035. Water underpins 70% of global GDP and 100% of food production, yet receives less than .01% of institutional capital. Based in Dubai, Delhi, Vaduz, and Munich, the Foundation pioneers blended, unsiloed financial strategies that combine cutting-edge technology, networked capital, and impact investment to close that gap.
